# Introduction to SIEM

## About This Room

> *Learn the fundamentals of SIEM and explore its features and functionality.*

**Learning Objectives:**
- Understand the different types of log sources
- Identify the limitations of working with isolated logs
- Recognise the importance of a SIEM solution
- Explore the features of a SIEM solution
- Learn various types of log sources and their ingestion into the SIEM
- Understand the process behind alerting and alert analysis
